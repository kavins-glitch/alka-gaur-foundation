About Us

Alka Gaur Foundation is dedicated to transforming lives through compassion, education, healthcare, and empowerment. Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities and create sustainable impact across society.

Our Story

What started as a small initiative to help a few families has grown into a powerful movement impacting thousands. Driven by compassion and purpose, the foundation continues to expand its reach and change lives every day.

2018: Foundation started
2020: Expanded into healthcare camps
2023: Reached 10,000+ beneficiaries

Our Initiatives

🍛 Food & Nutrition

Providing meals to ensure no one sleeps hungry.

📚 Education

Empowering children through access to quality education.

🏥 Healthcare

Organizing free health camps and medical support.

👩 Women Empowerment

Skill development and financial independence programs.
