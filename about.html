About Us

Alka Gaur Foundation is more than an organization — it is a movement driven by compassion, purpose, and the belief that every life deserves dignity. We work at the grassroots level to uplift underprivileged communities through food support, education, healthcare, and empowerment initiatives. Every step we take is towards creating a future where no one is left behind.

Our Story

What began as a small effort to help a few families has grown into a mission impacting thousands of lives. The foundation was built on a simple yet powerful idea — that real change starts when we care enough to act. From feeding the hungry to educating children and supporting women, our journey has been fueled by kindness, resilience, and the unwavering support of people like you.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities by providing access to basic necessities, education, and opportunities — enabling them to build a better, self-sustained future.

Our Vision

A world where every individual, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to live with dignity, hope, and purpose.

2018: Foundation established with a vision to serve communities
2020: Expanded initiatives to healthcare and education
2023: Impacted 10,000+ lives across multiple programs

Our Initiatives

🍛 Food & Nutrition

We provide nutritious meals and ration kits to families struggling with hunger.

📚 Education

We support children with education, school supplies, and opportunities to grow.

🏥 Healthcare

We organize free health camps and ensure access to basic medical care.

👩 Women Empowerment

We empower women through skill development and financial independence programs.

Why We Exist

In a world full of inequality, we exist to bridge the gap between privilege and need. We believe that even the smallest act of kindness can create a ripple effect of change.
