About the Foundation

A Vision Born from Compassion

"

"No woman should be allowed to compromise with her self-respect and dignity. Together, we can make a difference — one heart, one community at a time."

— Dr. Alka Gaur, Founder

Dr. Alka Gaur is a distinguished medical professional, entrepreneur, and passionate patron of arts and culture. When she founded the Alka Gaur Foundation, she faced profound skepticism — but her determination never wavered. Today, the foundation operates with a holistic lifecycle approach, supporting not just children but entire families for sustainable, long-term change across Rajasthan and beyond.

She is the visionary behind the Jaipur Artist Festival and Lok Kala Sangam 2026 — graced by Deputy CM Diya Kumari, affirming state support for women-led social innovation.

What We Do

Six Pillars of Lasting Impact

Six interconnected programs creating holistic, lasting change — from a child's first meal to a woman's first business. Each pillar reinforces the others for maximum community transformation.

Pillar 01
🍽️
Food & Nutrition

Ensuring no child, elderly person, or family sleeps hungry. Through our flagship "Food for All" campaign, we distribute hot meals, ration kits, and nutritional supplements across Jaipur and surrounding communities — spreading dignity alongside sustenance.

Education

Breaking generational poverty through knowledge. We provide textbooks, scholarships, digital devices, and school infrastructure — while running career guidance, mentorship, and digital literacy programs for underserved students across Rajasthan.

Healthcare & Wellness

Bringing quality healthcare to doorsteps. Free medical camps, dental check-ups, mental health initiatives, and preventive awareness programs — ensuring that good health is a right, not a privilege, for every family we serve.

The Beginning
Foundation Established in Jaipur

Dr. Alka Gaur founded the Alka Gaur Foundation with a clear mission: uplift underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and empowerment. Registered under 12A & 80G from the very start.

🍽️
First Campaign
Food for All Campaign Launched

The flagship meal distribution program begins. Thousands of hot meals delivered to children, elderly, and BPL families across Jaipur. The model scales rapidly with community trust and support.

👩‍💼
Scaling Impact
Women Empowerment Programs Begin

Vocational training centers established for rural women. The first cohort graduates and starts earning independently — changing families' financial trajectories for generations to come.

🏛️
Recognition

Official recognition from NITI Aayog and CSR-1 registration opens doors to corporate partnerships, dramatically expanding the foundation's reach across Rajasthan and beyond.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about donating, volunteering, and partnering with us.



Ask Us Anything →
How do I get a tax exemption certificate? ▾
We issue an official 80G tax exemption certificate within 7 working days of your donation. You receive 50% tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Donations above ₹2,000 must be made via cheque, bank transfer, or digital modes (not cash) to qualify.
How can my company partner for CSR? ▾
We are CSR-1 registered and your contribution is eligible under Schedule VII of the Companies Act 2013. Contact our partnerships team to schedule a call. We design a custom program aligned with your CSR goals and provide full compliance documentation for annual filings.
How can I volunteer with the foundation? ▾
We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds — doctors, educators, trainers, photographers, social workers, and more. Fill the contact form specifying "Volunteering" as your purpose and our team will match you with upcoming programs where your skills are most needed.
Is the foundation officially registered and verified? ▾
Absolutely. We hold 12AB & 80G registrations (Income Tax Act), NITI Aayog registration, CSR-1 registration, and ISO certification. Fully compliant with all Indian NGO regulations. All certifications are available for review upon request.
Can I direct my donation to a specific program? ▾
Yes! You can direct your donation to any of our six pillars: Food & Nutrition, Education, Healthcare, Women Empowerment, Environmental Sustainability, or Rehabilitation & Cancer Care. Specify your preference when contacting us.
How does the foundation report impact to donors? ▾
We practise radical transparency. Major donors and CSR partners receive quarterly impact reports with beneficiary counts, outcomes, photographs, and SROI metrics. All donors receive an impact summary with their tax receipt. We also share updates via social media and newsletter.
Where is the foundation located and what areas does it serve? ▾
Our office is at Plot B-136, Kirti Nagar, Tonk Road, Jaipur, Rajasthan – 302015. We primarily serve communities across Jaipur and Rajasthan. CSR partnerships can be designed for specific geographies across India — contact us to discuss your requirements.
