About the Foundation

A Vision Born from
Compassion

"No woman should be allowed to compromise with her self-respect and dignity. Together, we can make a difference."

— Dr. Alka Gaur, Founder

Dr. Alka Gaur is a distinguished medical professional, entrepreneur, and cultural visionary. When she founded the Alka Gaur Foundation, she faced skepticism — but her determination to serve the community never wavered. Today, the foundation operates with a holistic lifecycle approach that supports children, families, and communities for long-term, sustainable change across Rajasthan and beyond.

She is also the visionary behind the Jaipur Artist Festival and the acclaimed Lok Kala Sangam 2026, where master artisans from across Rajasthan showcased the soul of the state — with the gracious presence of Deputy Chief Minister Diya Kumari affirming government support.

What We Do

Our Programs &
Focus Areas

Six interconnected pillars of action that create holistic, lasting change for underprivileged communities — from the first meal to lifelong empowerment.

🍽️
Pillar 01
Food & Nutrition

Ensuring no child, elderly person, or family sleeps hungry. Through our Food for All campaign, we distribute meals, ration kits, and nutritional essentials to thousands of beneficiaries across Jaipur.

Meal Distribution Ration Kits Nutrition Drives Hunger Relief
📚
Pillar 02
Education

Breaking the cycle of poverty through knowledge. We provide books, scholarships, digital tools, and school infrastructure support while offering career guidance and digital literacy programs to underprivileged students.

Scholarships Digital Literacy School Infrastructure Career Guidance
🏥
Pillar 03
Healthcare & Wellness

Bringing quality healthcare to those who need it most. From free medical camps and dental check-ups to mental health initiatives and women's health programs — wellness is a right, not a privilege.

Free Medical Camps Dental Care Mental Health Women's Health
👩‍💼
Pillar 04
Women Empowerment

From skill development to entrepreneurship mentorship — we empower rural women to build independent livelihoods. Our vocational training programs cover tailoring, handicrafts, and business development.

Vocational Training Entrepreneurship Handicrafts Business Mentorship
🌿
Pillar 05
Environmental Sustainability

Protecting the planet for future generations through tree plantation drives, waste management awareness, and eco-friendly practice campaigns that engage communities and schools.

Tree Plantation Waste Management Eco Awareness Green Initiatives
💜
Pillar 06
Rehabilitation & Care

Rebuilding lives with care and compassion. Our rehabilitation programs provide dignified support, life coaching, and skill-building for mentally challenged women — and nutritional, emotional, and medical aid for cancer patients.

Cancer Care Life Coaching Disability Support Emotional Aid

Lok Kala Sangam 2026 — Celebrating Rajasthan's Living Heritage

In 2026, the Alka Gaur Foundation proudly hosted Lok Kala Sangam — a landmark event that brought master artisans from across Rajasthan together, showcasing traditional sculptures, handcrafted wooden artifacts, and the rich indigenous art forms of the state. The event was graced by Deputy Chief Minister Diya Kumari, reaffirming government support for women-led social initiatives and sustainable livelihoods.
